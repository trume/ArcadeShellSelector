# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, constructive, and professional collaboration.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be precise and respectful in technical discussions.
- Assume good intent, but challenge ideas with evidence.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and outcomes.
- Help keep discussions useful for contributors of different experience levels.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language
- Repeated bad-faith arguments or disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that makes participation unsafe or hostile

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If someone is violating this code of conduct, contact the maintainer privately if possible. Include links, screenshots, or other relevant context.
